Immersive music & art experiences

Church of Woo is an artist-built listening sanctuary where immersive sound, tactile bass, ceremony, and live performance come together as a single art experience. Guests step into a designed environment, sit inside the sound, and feel the music move through the body.

What makes the experience searchable

  • Immersive music and art experience for San Francisco and Bay Area audiences
  • 360-degree listening with an 11-speaker surround sound environment
  • Crescendo tactile bass floor for full-body vibration and deep listening
  • Artist-led ceremony, opera, electronic music, and experimental performance

For guests and partners

The experience is built for audiences, venues, galleries, and private hosts looking for something closer to an immersive listening space than a standard stage show. Explore the media package or contact the collective for upcoming events.
