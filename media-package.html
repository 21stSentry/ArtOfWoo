Sonic Bloom by Woo Art Collective

Immersive music and art experience for Bay Area venues and events

Sonic Bloom is a portable immersive listening environment for galleries, festivals, clubs, cultural institutions, and private events. It combines spatial sound, tactile bass, artist-built atmosphere, ceremony, and live performance inside a self-contained installation.

What venues receive

  • Immersive 360-degree listening sessions with an 11-speaker sound system
  • A tactile bass floor that lets guests feel the music through the body
  • Artist-led performances and ceremony-driven audience framing
  • A turnkey installation with load-in, operation, and strike handled by the team

Best fit

The experience is designed for venues that want a memorable immersive music and art activation rather than a standard stage show. It works especially well for Bay Area art spaces, listening events, branded activations, and intimate culture-forward programming.
